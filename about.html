About

I'm a hobby photographer from Gaspésie, Québec. Most of my work comes from moments spent by the sea or close to nature — rolling tides, changing light, transforming weather, and details that sometimes go unnoticed.

Photography has become a way of paying attention, connecting with the place I call home and with other encounters.

This portfolio is a growing collection of those stories. Thank you for stopping by.
