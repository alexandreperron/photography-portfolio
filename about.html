About

I'm a hobby photographer from Gaspésie, Québec. Most of my work comes from moments spent near the sea or close to nature.

Photography allows me to share some of the incredible scenes I witness around coastal villages — from rolling tides, changing light and transforming weather to stillness and details that might go unnoticed.

This portfolio is a growing collection of those stories. Thank you for stopping by.
