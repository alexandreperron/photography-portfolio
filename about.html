About

Hobby photographer based in Gaspésie, Québec.

Alexandre Perron — Gaspésie, Québec

Welcome to my online portfolio, where I share my favourite visual stories.

I most often document changing landscapes, breathing coastlines and thriving nature around Gaspésie.

Most of my photography happens while walking and exploring with my dog Apollo.

Always amazed by our surroundings, I use my photography to share some of what we observe and experience at home and elsewhere.

Thank you for taking the time to stop by.
